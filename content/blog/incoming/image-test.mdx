# Dark Mode Image Treatment Strategies

When displaying images with light backgrounds on dark-themed websites, we need special approaches to avoid the jarring visual effect. Here are some different strategies for treating images in dark mode:

## When to Use Each Strategy

- **Default/Subtle**: Good for most images with light backgrounds
- **Frame**: Best for data visualizations, charts, or diagrams where color accuracy is important
- **Vignette**: Works well for photos or images where you want to maintain the original look
- **Dim**: Best for very bright images or screenshots with white backgrounds
- **Invert**: Use sparingly for UI screenshots or technical diagrams where contrast is more important than color accuracy
